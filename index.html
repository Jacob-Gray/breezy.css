What is breezy.css?

Breezy is a lightweight CSS Library for modern browsers. Sizing only ~10kb, it includes a grid layout, form elements, buttons, links, headings, headers, and more. Breezy is made to be more of a helper framework, and can be easily changed to fit your needs.

And, breezy has some nice pre-styled elements, so you can be lazy ♥

Headers

Headers are the usually placed at the top of pages, hence the name. There are 5 different header classes. The first 4, .b-header--25, .b-header--50, .b-header--75, .b-header--100 are calculated by the browsers height. .b-header--25, for example, will be 25 percent of the browsers height:
